Our Journey

Full List of Achievements

Every recognition and partnership is a story of commitment and excellence.

May 6, 2025

50th Anniversary – UAE Armed Forces

To mark five decades of valor and unity, Ciggyspot Tobacco Trading FZE joined in the nation’s celebration of the UAE Armed Forces 50th Anniversary.
The commemorative event recognized the leadership of H.H. Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Al Nahyan and H.H. Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, and honored the dedication of Emirati soldiers who have secured the nation's prosperity and security.
Our partnership in these national moments reinforces our pride in contributing to the UAE’s remarkable journey.

Feb 10, 2025

Certificate of Appreciation

Ciggyspot Tobacco Trading FZE was honored with a Certificate of Appreciation from Al Diwan Al Malaki Magazine, recognizing our company’s valuable contributions to community initiatives.
The award, received by Mr. Hakkim Akkallath, reflects our commitment to building strong, impactful partnerships across the UAE. We are grateful for the trust and recognition shown by such prestigious institutions.
This achievement inspires us to further elevate our standards in business and social responsibility.

Jun 28, 2025

2025: The Year of Community

In 2025, the UAE announced "The Year of Community" to champion inclusion, harmony, and shared growth. Ciggyspot proudly supported initiatives aligned with this vision, helping foster unity and social progress.
Our involvement ranged from awareness campaigns to hands-on engagement with local communities, supporting the leadership’s goal of building a brighter, more connected future for all residents and citizens.

Jun 30, 2025

Cigior Premium Blends Launch

The launch of Cigior Premium Blends set a new benchmark for excellence in tobacco. Our new line delivers not just superior taste, but also upholds the heritage and artistry of fine tobacco blending.
This milestone reflects our dedication to innovation, quality, and the evolving preferences of discerning consumers across the region.
Cigior now stands as a proud symbol of refinement and tradition in the premium tobacco sector.

Cigior Tobacco Trading FZE stands as a leading name in the premium tobacco industry, specializing in the global trade, distribution, and export of high-quality cigarette products. Experience reliability, compliance, and a commitment to excellence with every partnership.
